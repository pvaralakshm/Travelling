Ananthagiri

At a distance of 26 km from Araku, 85 km from Vizag & 660 km from Hyderabad,
Anantagiri is a lovely tiny hill station between Vizag and Araku Valley. It is one of the top
tourist places to visit in Araku Valley and is also among the top hill station in Andhra Pradesh
apart from being a popular weekend getaway from Vizag city.

#2 of 20 Places to Visit in Araku Valley

Distance (From Araku Valley): 30 Kms

Trip Duration (Including Travel): 1-2 Hours

Transportation Options: Bus / Cab

Borra Caves

At a distance of 36 km from Araku, 88 km from Vizag & 662 km from Hyderabad,
Borra Caves are one million year-old limestone caves and the deepest caves in the country.
Situated in Anantagiri hills of Andhra Pradesh

#1 of 20 Places to Visit in Araku Valley

Distance (From Araku Valley): 36 Kms

Trip Duration (Including Travel): 3-4 Hours

Transportation Options: Bus / Cab

Tribal Museum

At a distance of 200 m from Araku Bus Station, Araku Tribal Museum is a wonderful
place which explores the tribal culture of Eastern Ghats. It is one of the top tourist
places to visit in Araku Valley.
